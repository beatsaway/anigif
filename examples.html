Basic Animation Effects Showcase

Translate

MOVE

Moving objects along X, Y, or Z axes. Essential for position-based animations.

Scale

SIZE

Growing or shrinking objects. Perfect for emphasis and attention-grabbing effects.

Rotate

SPIN

Spinning around center point or custom pivot. Great for loading indicators.

Skew

TILT

Slanting or tilting objects at angles. Adds dynamic perspective to elements.

Stretch

PULL

Elongating in one direction while compressing in another. Creates elastic feel.

Squash

FLAT

Flattening for impact or weight simulation. Classic animation principle.

Bend

BEND

Rectangle morphing from straight edges to curved corners. Shows border bending effect.

Twist

3D

Rotating different parts at different rates. Creates 3D flip effects.

Wave

Creating sine wave distortions. Perfect for water or flag effects.

Bulge

GROW

Expanding outward from center point. Great for button hover effects.

Fade

FADE

Gradually changing transparency. Most common transition effect.

Dissolve

BLUR

Pixelated fade effect using blur. Creates dreamy transition feel.

Wipe

WIPE

Revealing or hiding with moving edge. Great for revealing content.

Follow Path

PATH

Moving along predefined curves. Perfect for complex motion trails.

Morph

MORPH

Smoothly changing between different shapes. Creates fluid transformations.

Draw On

Revealing lines as if being drawn. Great for SVG line animations.

Color Shift

COLOR

Transitioning between different hues. Creates vibrant, dynamic effects.

Gradient Animation

GRAD

Moving color gradients. Perfect for modern, flowing backgrounds.

Stroke Weight

BORDER

Changing line thickness. Great for emphasizing outlines.

Bounce

JUMP

Elastic collision simulation. Adds playful, energetic feel.

Swing

SWING

Pendulum motion. Perfect for hanging or dangling objects.

Gravity

FALL

Falling with acceleration. Simulates realistic dropping motion.

Spring

SPRING

Oscillating back to rest position. Creates natural, bouncy feel.

Particles

EMIT

Spawning multiple small elements. Perfect for magical effects.

Explosion

BOOM

Radial burst patterns. Creates dramatic impact effects.
